I'm a researcher at the Department of Computer Science and Systems Engineering (DIIS) of the University of Zaragoza (Spain). I studied a Bachelor's in Computer Science at University of Zaragoza and a Master's in Robotics, Graphics and Computer Vision at University of Zaragoza and University of Technology Sydney (Australia). My research interests include the areas of machine learning, computer graphics and efficiency.
